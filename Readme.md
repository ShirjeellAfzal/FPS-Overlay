# ✨ Simple FPS Overlay ✨
### *Minimalist • Customizable • Lightweight*

A clean and elegant FPS display mod for **Minecraft 1.21 (Fabric)**. Designed for players who want to keep track of their performance without cluttering their screen.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🖱️ **Interactive Position Editor**
Forget manual coordinates. Press **'U'**, click **Edit Position**, and simply **drag and drop** the overlay anywhere on your screen. It features smart-snapping to corners for a perfect fit!

### 🎨 **Total Visual Control**
*   **Dual Color System**: Choose from high-visibility **Presets** (Red, Green, Blue, etc.) or type any **Hex Code** for infinite color possibilities.
*   **Dynamic FPS Color**: Let the mod speak to you. Text color shifts automatically from **Green** (60+) to **Yellow** (30+) to **Red** (<30) based on your performance.
*   **Background Styling**: Toggle a sleek background box with a dedicated **Opacity Slider** (0-100%) to match your HUD's aesthetic.

### 📐 **Flexible Layouts**
*   **Display Styles**: Switch between a clean **Normal** look or a professional **Brackets** style `[ FPS: 144 ]`.
*   **Text Scaling**: A smooth slider allows you to scale the text from **50% to 500%** of its original size.
*   **Advanced Stats**: Optional toggle to show your **Min/Avg/Max** FPS alongside the current counter.

---

## 🎮 Controls
| Keybind | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **`U`** | Open Settings Menu |
| **`Mouse Drag`** | Move Overlay (in Editor Mode) |
| **`ESC`** | Save & Close |
